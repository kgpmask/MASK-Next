Declaration

During the summer vacation of 2024, MASK conducted its first online art competition; you can probably guess the theme. Yes, it was Bidoof! In celebration of Bidoof Day, which was on 1st July, we organized an art competition celebrating Bidoof.
The competition was divided into two sections, Traditional Art and Digital Art, and registration started on the 20th of May. The competition Deadline was 20th June, and by the end, we had received many submissions. After a long and rigorous grading process, the results were finally declared on 1st July, Bidoof Day.
